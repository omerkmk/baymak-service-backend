About Us

Baymak Technical Service is a leading provider of professional technical service solutions for air conditioning, heating, and cooling systems. With years of experience in the industry, we are committed to delivering high-quality service and maintenance solutions to our customers.

Our expert team of certified technicians provides comprehensive services including installation, maintenance, repair, and emergency support for all types of HVAC systems. We specialize in servicing Baymak brand products as well as other major brands, ensuring your comfort systems operate at peak efficiency.

Our services include annual maintenance contracts, warranty services, emergency repairs, installation of new systems, and technical consultations. We use state-of-the-art equipment and follow industry best practices to ensure your devices receive the highest quality care.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer flexible scheduling, transparent pricing, and reliable service guarantees. Whether you need routine maintenance or urgent repairs, our team is ready to assist you with professional and efficient service solutions.
